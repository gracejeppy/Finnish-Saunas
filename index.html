Finnish Saunas

Step into the realm of relaxation and heat with Finnish saunas – the ultimate chill zone for body and mind! Picture yourself in a cozy wooden cabin, surrounded by the serene beauty of Finland's landscapes. As you step inside, feel the warmth enveloping you like a comforting hug from nature itself.

In Finnish saunas, sweating is not just a pastime, it's a ritual, a tradition passed down through generations. It's where Finns escape the hustle and bustle of modern life to unwind, detoxify, and rejuvenate. It's a place where time slows down, and worries melt away in the soothing steam.

Embrace the Finnish "löyly" – the magical moment when water is splashed onto the hot sauna rocks, releasing a burst of steam that tingles your skin and invigorates your senses. It's like being hugged by a cloud! But it's not just about the heat – Finnish saunas are a social affair too. Imagine swapping stories and laughter with friends or strangers, all while basking in the gentle warmth and camaraderie of the sauna.

And let's not forget the post-sauna plunge into icy waters or a refreshing roll in the snow – a shock to the system that leaves you feeling alive, invigorated, and ready to conquer the world (or at least the rest of your day)! So, whether you're a seasoned sauna-goer or a first-timer, embrace the Finnish sauna experience – a hot ticket to relaxation, rejuvenation, and a whole lot of fun!

Sauna Must-Have Check List:

  1. A yummy sauna scent to pour on coals
    • Peppermint
    • Eucalyptus
    • Lavender
  2. Saunavasta (sauna whisk)
    • a popular Finnish tradition
    • User hits thier friends with birch branches to improve blood circulation
  3. A yummy post-sauna treat
    • Mämmi (rye flour and malt pudding)
    • Korvapuusti (Finnish hybrid of a dry pastry and cinnamon roll)
    • Salmiakki ice cream (gross salty black liquorice ice cream)
  4. No Clothes
